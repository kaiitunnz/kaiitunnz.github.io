About

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at the National University of Singapore (NUS), supported by the President's Graduate Fellowship. My research interests include machine learning systems, big data systems, and agentic AI, with a focus on how to execute complex multi-step / multi-agent LLM workflows efficiently on modern hardware.

Before NUS, I completed a B.Sc. in Computer Science (double major in Electrical Engineering) at KAIST with a 4.20/4.30 GPA. My applied work also includes causal modeling for banking analytics and production ML pipelines.
